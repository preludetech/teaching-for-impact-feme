    Template 1: System Map¶

    Version¶

    • v0 (current understanding) / v1 / v2

    Instructions¶

    Map your training as part of a real-world system.

    You will use¶

    • Your current training description
    • Use 1 learner constraint from Template 2 (if available)

    If you already have a training (recommended)¶

    Your current training¶

    • What is the training called?
    • What does it aim to do?

    System mapping¶

    List key elements:

    Actors - Who is involved? (learners, trainers, institutions, funders, communities)

    Inputs - What resources are used? (time, tools, data, funding)

    Activities - What actually happens in the training?

    Outputs - What is produced immediately?

    Outcomes - What changes for participants?

    Wider system influences - What else affects outcomes? (context, policy, infrastructure, culture)

    Reality check¶

    • What assumptions are you making about this system?
    • Where are the biggest risks or uncertainties?
    • Use 1 learner constraint from Template 2 to validate assumptions

    If you are creating a new training¶

    Map the system you are designing for using the same structure above.

    Translation to your learners¶

    • How does this system reflect your learners’ real context?
    • What factors matter most for them?

    Context check¶

    • What constraints affect this system?
    • What is outside your control?

    Iteration¶

    • v0 = draft
    • v1 = revised
    • v2 = final
    • Update previous templates if needed

    Reuse in later sections¶

    • Template 2 (Learner Reality Mapping)
    • Template 4 (Theory of Change)
    • Template 5 (Learning Outcomes)

    Reflection¶

    • What did you discover about where your training sits in a broader system?

    Template 5: Learning Outcomes¶

    Version¶

    • v0 / v1 / v2

    Instructions¶

    Define what learners will be able to DO.

    You will use¶

    • Your theory of change from Template 4
    • Use 1 learner constraint from Template 2

    If you already have a training (recommended)¶

    Current outcomes¶

    List existing outcomes.

    Diagnose¶

    For each outcome: - Is it observable? - Is it measurable? - Is it realistic in your context? - Use 1 learner constraint from Template 2

    Rewrite outcomes¶

    Write improved outcomes using: - Action verbs - Clear context - Observable behaviour

    If you are creating a new training¶

    Write 3–5 learning outcomes.

    Translation to your learners¶

    • Are these outcomes achievable for your learners?
    • What needs adjusting?

    Context check¶

    • Do constraints affect these outcomes?
